From young, I have always been drawing and writing poems and articles. Then, I developed the skill for public speaking, the secret is to be confident, you can be scared of an audience but you must hide it and know your lines thoroughly. For my scripts, I draw from memories and inspirations, whatever I've experienced, seen, and felt, I learn to put into words, it really is an amazing way to express yourself. For my designs, it's all trial-and-error, it's a long process but it's worth it in the end. I am proud of all my works because I know the progression that I made from the beginning til the end of a work, and to me, it is all good work.
